# Hugging Face  
**The AI Community Building the Future**

## About Hugging Face  
Hugging Face is a premier collaboration platform dedicated to the machine learning (ML) community. Founded in 2016 and headquartered in Paris, France, Hugging Face fosters innovation by enabling users worldwide to discover, share, and develop models, datasets, and AI applications. As a pivotal force in the AI revolution, Hugging Face empowers ML engineers, scientists, and enthusiasts to collaborate openly and ethically to shape the future of artificial intelligence.

---

## Core Offerings

### 1. Hugging Face Hub  
- **Models**: Access and contribute to over 1 million machine learning models across modalities including text, image, video, audio, and 3D.  
- **Datasets**: Explore and share 250,000+ datasets curated for diverse AI tasks, enabling rapid experimentation and development.  
- **Spaces**: Host and deploy interactive ML applications directly on the platform, with thousands of community-powered demos and projects.

### 2. Community & Collaboration  
- A vibrant, global community supports learning, experimentation, and sharing of best practices.  
- Open-source philosophy encourages transparency and democratization of AI technology.

### 3. Enterprise Solutions  
- **Team Plan**: Starting at $20/user/month, designed for growing teams needing secure collaboration, single sign-on (SSO), granular access control, detailed analytics, and advanced compute options.  
- **Enterprise Plan**: Custom pricing from $50/user/month, offering personalized onboarding, highest storage and API limits, managed billing, legal compliance, and priority support.  
- Features include private dataset viewing, configurable security policies, audit logs, and resource management for optimized organizational control.

### 4. Pro Account  
- Personal subscriptions at $9/month enhance individual user capabilities with increased private storage, higher inference credits, priority compute resources, and enhanced publishing capabilities.
